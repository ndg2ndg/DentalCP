HIPAA Compliance

HIPAA-Compliant Practice

Dental Center Pediatrics & Orthodontics is fully committed to protecting the privacy of your health information in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) and all applicable regulations.

As a covered healthcare entity, we maintain strict administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your Protected Health Information (PHI). Our staff receives regular HIPAA training, and access to patient records is limited to authorized personnel only.

You have the right to access your records, request amendments, and receive an accounting of disclosures. To exercise any of these rights, please contact our office directly.

SMS / Text Message Communications

We use SMS text messaging to send appointment-related communications to patients and their guardians. By providing your mobile phone number and consenting, you agree to receive the following types of messages:

  • Appointment reminders
  • Appointment confirmations and scheduling updates
  • Recall notifications (routine checkup reminders)
  • Secure message alerts from our care team

Consent is always voluntary. You are never required to consent to SMS messages as a condition of receiving care at our practice. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent. Reply HELP for assistance at any time.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your care schedule. For questions about SMS messaging, contact us at office@dentalcp.com or (508) 830-1212.

How We Collect and Use Patient Information

We collect information from patients and their parents or guardians in order to provide dental and orthodontic care. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Contact information (name, address, phone number, email)
  • Date of birth and demographic information
  • Dental and medical history
  • Insurance and billing information
  • Appointment and treatment records

This information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide, coordinate, and improve patient care
  • Process insurance claims and billing
  • Send appointment reminders and clinical communications
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is only disclosed to third parties as required to provide your care (e.g., specialists, labs, insurance providers) or as required by law.

Online Forms and Digital Communications

When you submit information through our online appointment request or records release forms, that data is transmitted securely to our office via a HIPAA-compliant communication channel. Form submissions are not stored in browser-side storage, third-party databases, or any unsecured location.

Our website does not use tracking pixels, behavioral advertising scripts, or third-party analytics tools that collect personally identifiable health information.
